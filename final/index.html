Historical Building

History

The towers are a World Heritage Site. Established in
China during the Ming Dynasty, the
last one was built in 1949.

Fucntion

It was used to protect the local population from
bandits, and to resist the war.

Feature

Kaiping Diaolou is a multi-storey building for defense.
And very diverse, some are a combination of
Chinese and Western architecture.
